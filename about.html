About Legends Guess

The daily game for football & basketball fans

Legends Guess is a free daily puzzle where you guess a mystery football (soccer) or basketball legend from the clues of their career — then, once you solve it, you get to watch that legend play. It's built by fans, for fans: quick to play, satisfying to solve, and a great excuse to rewatch the greats.

How the game works

1
Pick your sport

⚽ Football or 🏀 Basketball — switch anytime.

2
Guess the legend

Clues reveal their clubs/teams, nation and era. Each wrong guess unlocks a new clue. You get 6 tries.

3
Watch them play 🎬

Solve it (or give up) and highlight buttons appear — their best moments on YouTube, TikTok and more.

Why we host no video

We believe creators and rights holders should keep every view and all the credit. So Legends Guess never hosts or re-uploads any clip. Every "Watch" button simply opens a live search on YouTube, TikTok or Instagram in a new tab, sending you to the original uploads. That keeps the game legal, always fresh, and unbreakable.

New puzzles daily

Each day brings a fresh daily puzzle in both sports. Keep your 🔥 streak going, share your result grid with friends, and use 🎲 Endless mode for unlimited practice. Our roster of legends spans eras and continents and keeps growing.

Independence

Legends Guess is an independent, unofficial fan project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with any league, club, player, or governing body. All names and trademarks belong to their respective owners.
