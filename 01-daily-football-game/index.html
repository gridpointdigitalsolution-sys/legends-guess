How to play ⚽

A mystery footballer is hidden behind their career path. You get 6 guesses.

You start with the player's position and first club. Every wrong guess unlocks the next clue — more clubs, then nationality, birth year, and finally the first letter of their name.

Guess in fewer tries for a better score, keep your daily 🔥 streak alive, and share your result grid with friends.

One new puzzle every day at midnight (your local time). Use 🎲 Practice Mode for unlimited games.
