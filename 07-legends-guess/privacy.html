Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Legends Guess ("we", "us", the "Site") is a free, fan-made daily puzzle game for football (soccer) and basketball fans, available at legendsguess.com. This policy explains what information we handle when you play, and your choices. We keep it deliberately minimal.

1. Information we collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect your name, email, or any personal profile to play the game.

  • Game progress & stats — your streak, games played, wins and puzzle history are stored only in your own browser (via localStorage). This never leaves your device and we cannot read it. Clearing your browser data removes it.
  • Basic technical data — like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically log standard request data (such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed) for security and to keep the Site running. We do not use this to identify you.

2. Cookies and advertising

We plan to show ads through Google AdSense to keep the game free. When ads are enabled:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For opting out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising, visit aboutads.info.

Until ads are activated, no advertising cookies are set by us.

3. Highlight videos (external links)

We host no video. "Watch them play" buttons open a live search on YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram in a new tab. Once you leave our Site, the privacy policy of that platform applies. All video views and credit stay with the original creators and rights holders.

4. Children's privacy

The Site is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your rights & choices

Because we store game stats only on your device and collect no personal profile, you stay in control: clear your browser storage to erase all local game data at any time. You can also block or delete cookies in your browser settings.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Site grows (for example, when advertising goes live). Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email GridpointDigitalSolution@gmail.com or see our Contact page.
