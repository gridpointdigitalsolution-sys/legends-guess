Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you

Legends Guess is run by an independent team of football and basketball fans. For anything at all — feedback, a bug, a legend we should add, a career detail to correct, a partnership, or an advertising enquiry — reach us by email and we'll get back to you.

✉ hello@legendsguess.com

Good reasons to email

  • Corrections — spotted a wrong club, era, or nationality in a puzzle? Tell us and we'll fix it fast. Accuracy matters to us.
  • Suggest a legend — want a particular player or era added to the roster? Send names.
  • Bugs & feedback — anything not working, or an idea to make the game better.
  • Advertising & partnerships — for sponsorship or ad enquiries.
  • Rights & takedowns — we host no video and only link to public searches, but if you're a rights holder with a concern, get in touch and we'll respond promptly.

Response time

We're a small team, so please allow a few days for a reply. We read every message.
