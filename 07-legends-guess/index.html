How to play 🏅

1. Pick a sport — ⚽ Football or 🏀 Basketball at the top.

2. Guess the legend — the cards show clues about their career (clubs/teams they played for, in order, plus nation and era), and you get a masked hint of their name showing the first letter and how many letters it has. Type a name in the box and press SUBMIT GUESS.

3. Wrong guess = new clue. Each miss unlocks the next clue. You have 6 guesses total.

4. Watch them play 🎬 — when you solve it (or press "Give up"), highlight buttons appear so you can watch their best moments instantly.

Come back daily for a new puzzle in each sport, keep your 🔥 streak, and use 🎲 Endless for unlimited games.

Legends Guess — the daily football & basketball legend puzzle

Legends Guess is a free daily guessing game for football (soccer) and basketball fans. Each day you get a new mystery legend to identify from clues about their career — the clubs or teams they played for, their nation and era, and a masked hint of their name. Solve the puzzle in six guesses and their best highlights unlock instantly. It plays in any browser on mobile and desktop, and you can install it as an app.

Test your knowledge across hundreds of football and basketball legends — from all-time greats to modern stars, spanning World Cups, the Champions League, the Premier League, LaLiga, Serie A, the NBA, EuroLeague and the women's game. Keep your daily streak, share your result, and play endless mode for unlimited rounds.

Frequently asked questions

Is Legends Guess free? Yes — completely free, no account needed, new daily puzzles in both sports plus an endless mode.

How do I play? Read the career clues, type a legend's name, and submit. Each wrong guess reveals a new clue; you get six tries. Solve it (or give up) and highlight buttons appear.

Where do the highlights come from? We host no video. "Watch" buttons open a live search on YouTube, TikTok or Instagram, so all views and credit stay with the original creators.

Which legends are included? Football and basketball greats across every era and continent — see the full list of legends.
